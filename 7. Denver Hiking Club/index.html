Denver Hiking Club

The Denver Hiking Club is committed to encouraging the exploration of the beautiful nature here in Denver, Colorado. We invite all individuals who love nature to join us. Once every two weeks, we gather to explore Denver’s the wonderful forests. We also provide resources for those interested, but unable to commit to our structured events.
